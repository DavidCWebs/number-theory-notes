Diophantine Equations
=====================

Theorem
Given integers _a_, _b_, _c_ where at least one of _a_ and _b_ ≠ 0, the Diophantine equation

_ax_ + _by_ = _c_

has a solution (where _x_ and _y_ are integers if and only if:

gcd(_a_, _b_) │ _c_.
